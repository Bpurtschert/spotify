Paste your Access Token and fetch your stats. No login required.

How to Get Your Spotify Access Token

  1. Go to https://developer.spotify.com/ and click “See it in action”.
  2. In the “Code” box, look at the very top where you see token.
  3. Copy everything inside the parentheses (without the quotes).
  4. Paste that value into the “Spotify Access Token” box above.

Note: Access tokens expire in about 1 hour. You’ll need to repeat these steps when it stops working.
